Mixt

MIXT is a personal project developed along with Joe Giglio to try out our first full WebApp using Ruby on Rails and streaming services' API. Being both lovers of music and Cassette Tapes, we decided to build a Vaporwave looking platform that allows users to create and customize digital mixtapes (~10 songs limit) that they can share with their friends and loved ones, or just keep them on their own digital shelves ¯\_(ツ)_/¯ who knows.
