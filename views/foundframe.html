Foundframe

FOUNDFRAME is a photography magazine & web application designed to help art lovers find and schedule visits to specialized galleries and exhibitions.


Backstory

As a lover of photography (and the arts in general), I am in the constant search of exhibitions happening around the city I'm living in. However, when I came to NYC, I realized there was not an easy way of finding about about showings, galleries, timetables or openings. I started developing FOUNDFRAME with a focus on photography, but with the possibility of making it scalable towards many more art forms and locations.
